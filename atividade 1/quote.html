“Look at my eyes, Faye. One of them is a fake because I lost it in an accident. Since then, I’ve been seeing the past in one eye and the present in the other”

This quote is from Spike Spiegel, the main character of the famous anime Cowboy Bebop. Spike is a character that has great problems about not living his present, because he is always thinking about all the things that already happened, and suffering for things he already lost.
